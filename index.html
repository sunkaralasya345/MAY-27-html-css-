Welcome to the Society Survey and Representative Election Management System. This digital portal is your centralized hub to participate in community surveys and cast secure, verifiable votes to elect your society's representatives.

Purpose of the survey and polling system:

The primary purpose of a Society Survey and Representative Election Management System is to digitise, streamline, and secure the processes of gathering community feedback and conducting democratic internal elections within a residential society or organization.
